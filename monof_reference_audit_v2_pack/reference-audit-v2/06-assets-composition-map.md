## Composition lessons

- Work images are real editorial images, not simple abstract boxes. Key visible work assets have rendered sizes around `1029×1008`, and additional work images around `672×400`.
- Partner logos are individual image marks, mostly 80×80 or 104×104, placed in a dense grid.
- Blog cards use image thumbnails around `304×233` inside 334×358 cards.
- Showreel has several image frames (`Scene #...`) around 334×234 and a centered play button.
- Footer/menu social icons are 40px dark/round/square buttons.
